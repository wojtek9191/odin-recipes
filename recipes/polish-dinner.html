Polish dinner

Absolute classic served in all Polish homes

Potatoes, pork chops and cucumbers. Sounds simple? It is simple and very delicious! This is a classic dinner that every Pole has eaten at some point in their lives. Most probably on Sundays.

Ingredients

  • pork chops
  • salt, pepper, garlic
  • 1 egg
  • bread crumbs
  • potatoes
  • dill
  • butter
  • cucumber
  • natural youghurt

Steps

  1. Pound pork chops with a meat mallet until very thin.
  2. Cut 1 garlic clove in half and rub it on the meat. Andd salt and pepper.
  3. Whisk 1 egg in a shallow bowl and place breadcrumbs in a separate shallow bowl.
  4. Dip chops in whisked eggs and coat them in breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-hih heat. Add breaded chops and cook until brown on both sides.
  6. Cook potatoes in separate pot. Once cooked, add 1 teaspoon of butter and chopped dill.
  7. Cut cucumber into slices and mix it in 2 tablespoons of natural youghurt with salt and pepper.
